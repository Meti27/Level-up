How to escape the endless loop of procastionation and become more focused, remove brain fog, remove anxiety&stress.

There is an scientifically proven method that i know seems to good to be true but it works.It might be a little hard to do but i promise you that after this detox you will feel so much better mentally.

Dopamine is a hormone that often is connected with motivation&pleasure. The reason why you dont have any motivation to do anything is because you have fried your dopamine receptors with short term pleasures such as:scrolling through your phone watching short videos,eating junk food,watching porn, etc.

Now to fix this we are going for a week eliminate the bad habits and implement some good ones.

  • Do not use your phone - for this week do not consume any social media content, don't watch any movies or shows and do not play videogames
  • Eat clean - stop eating junk food cut our all processed food and sugar, drink only water
  • Do not masturbate or watch any adult films
  • Read at least 30 minutes daily - preferably read an non-fiction book so you can learn while reading
  • Meditate at least 10 minutes daily -meditation is not a religious act its simply clearing your mind and make u able to focus better, it is also the biggest contributor to removing anxiety
  • Go out - go for a walk or hike in nature connect with nature, if you aren't that type of person go socialize with friends

After the end of the week you can go back to using your phone but now do it at a more controlled rate and dont forget never get too comfortable because you will start the deppresive loop again and the detox after a month will be worthless.Keep doing the good habits and completly remove porn and short videos.

The benefits you will feel after this detox: way better sleep quality,more energy levels,more focus,less anxiety,more motivation,etc.

Clean space = Clear mind

Remember to always keep a clean space whether that be at work or at home.A clean space represent a clear mind, having a clean space will greatly improve your mental clarity and you will be able to focus more at the task that u should be doing, it helps you work more effectively.

For me my bedroom is also my work space so its a two in one but whatever places you stay longer to make sure to always keep it clean and tidy. Invest in your bedroom&working space buy a few air cleaning plants like: Snake plant, Peace lily, English ivy,Lavender,etc. Maybe put up posters that you can look at and remember why you started to keep you motivated.

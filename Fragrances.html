Fragrance Guide

You want to be the type of guy that everyone talks about how good you smell. People should know you've entered a room before they even turn around. This is the power of having a signature scent.
Fragrance is a personal statement, as it's all about how the scent smells on you specifically and how it's perceived by others. Finding your signature scent can make you turn heads and make a lasting impression that says “I have arrived”.
In this article, we're going to go through fragrance tips, how to build a fragrance collection, fragrance suggestions for different price points and how to find your signature scent.

Must know

1. Most important thing before using a fragrance is to be clean and not smell bad, if you try to mask the bad smell with a fragrance your body odor will mix with the fragrance and leave you with a bad smell of the fragrance.

2. Less is more- if you are using a quality fragrance most depending on the sillage u need from 3 to 7 sprays max.

3. Use the right fragrance for the right moment- if it's a hot day of summer don't go spraying 7 sprays of "stronger with you" the smell will be overpowering and just won't feel right, knowing what fragrance to use on different occasions is key.

4. More expensive is not always better- you can find plenty of clones of niche fragrances with the 1/3 of the price, don't go blind buying everything unless you are rich then just do what you want.

How to build a well rounded fragrance collection

To have a well rounded fragrance collection you are going to need 4 signature fragrances.

  • Go to everyday fragrance - in this category are mostly the blue fragrances that u can wear at any occasion: work, school, gym,etc.
  • A loud fragrance - this is the fragrance that you are going to use going out to party, this fragrance is goind to have powerful sillage.
  • Date night fragrance - this is going to be a sweet fragrance that you are going to use when going out on a date, should have 8+ longevity&powerful fragrance.
  • Special occasion fragrance - this going to be that fragrance that u rarely pull out, an inoffensive but shows authority type of fragrance.

Fragrance recommendations

Out of each fragrance type im going to give different options starting from priciest to cheapest

  • Everyday fragrance - Blue de Channel,Ysl-y,Versace Dylan blue,Missioni pour homme.
  • Loud fragrance - LV Ombre Nomade, Dior sauvage Elixir, Mancera Red Tobbaco, JPG Ultramale, Afnan 9 Pm.
  • Date night fragrance - Alexandria II Xerjoff, Tom Ford Noir extreme, Emporio Armani Stronger with you, Khamrah Lattafa Perfumes.
  • Special occasion fragrance - Layton Parfums de Marly, Tom ford Ombre Leather, Tom Ford Oud Wood, JPG le parfum, Détour Noir Al Haramain.

Extra fragrance tips

A very important thing we haven't mentioned yet is that fragrances also have seasons that they are most suitable for, instead of making a whole list of each season and what fragrances shine in that season i will let you do your own research.
Go to Fragrantica and look at which season the fragrance is most suitable for here you also have longevity&sillage and reviews from people.

Where to spray

Depending on how strong the fragrance is you should spray 3 to 7 times now the main spraying points are going to be: behind each ear, on the neck , wrist or elbow, collar each side. Follow this guide and please do not overspray a fragrance is meant to be noticed but not suffocate people.
An extra tip to make your fragrance last much longer and perform better is to put vaseline on the spray points before spraying.

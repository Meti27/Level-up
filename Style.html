How to choose your style

Dapper and sophisticated, the world of men's style is a realm of endless possibilities. From timeless classics to bold fashion statements, it's a canvas where individuality meets sartorial elegance. In this style article, we delve into the art of mastering the details, exploring the nuances that elevate an outfit from ordinary to extraordinary. Whether you're a seasoned style enthusiast or just starting to refine your personal aesthetic, this article will help you find and embrace your style.

The Timeless Wardrobe

If you are not sure of what style asthetic suits you i suggest you first build a Timeless Wardrobe. The Timeless Wardore means having clothing that never go out of style and that you can make multiple outfits from. Here are the essenitals for a timeless wardrobe:

  • A Black and a White T-shirts- If you have a nice physique get slim-fit ones. Where to buy: Uniqlo, Mango, Zara, etc.
  • Blue Denim Jeans- Don't wear jeans with holes or skinny jeans, get nice fit straight quality jeans. Where to buy:Levi's, Arket, Zara,etc.
  • Black Pants- Get a pair that fits you nicely and that is high quality. Where to buy: River island, Zara, Nordstrom, etc.
  • Cream or Khaki Pants- Choose whatever color suits you better. Where to buy: River Island, Zara, Nordstrom, etc.
  • White Oxford Shirt- Choose one that fits you nicely and high quality. Where to buy: Ralp Lauren, Zara, Mango, etc.
  • Polo t-shirt- Choose one that fits you nicely and high quality. Where to buy: Ralp Lauren, Luca Faloni, Zara, etc.
  • Knit/Wool Sweater- Choose one that fits you nicely and high quality. Where to buy: Abercrombie&Fitch, Arket, Zara, etc.
  • Black/Grey hoodie- Choose one that fits you nicely and high quality. Where to buy: Abercrombie&Fitch,Uniqlo, Asos, etc.
  • Black Jackets- You are going to need 2 black jackets one for when its colder like a puffer jacket and one for warmer weathers like a bomber jacket. Where to buy: Zara, River island, Pull&Bear, etc.
  • Overcoat- You can get an black/grey/beige overcoat. Where to get: L'Estrange London, Hugo Boss, Zara,etc.
  • White Sneakers- You can get nike AF1, Rebook club c, New balance 550, etc.

Now that you have the essentials that every man must have you can add other pieces of clothing one by one and try experiment with different styles.

How to choose a specific style asthetic that you like.

To choose a specific style that you like use social media, like: Pinterest, Instagram, Tik Tok.
But just because a specific style looks good on some online model doesn't mean it will look good on you. There are a few factors that you must put into action when choosing your style.

  • Body- just because a slim fit T-shirt loos good on a muscular model doesn't mean will look the same on you if you are super skinny or fat. First is important you work on your body but for now use more oversized clothes.
  • Skin tone- it's important to take into consideration the color of your skin before building a wardrobe, choose colors that compliment you skintone. Here's an article to help you know what colors suits you best: HOW TO DETERMINE WHAT COLOR LOOKS BEST ON YOUR SKIN TONE
  • Hair- hair is the same as skintone it can make a big difference to your style. This is fairly easy to know just look at the models that have your hairstyle&color and try to copy them with how they dress and style colors.
  • Personality- if you are an extrovert and outgoing person choosing to wear very dark colors just doesn't fit with your personality and vice versa. Your profession also plays a big role in your style, if you work in an office a more sophisticated look will suit you better, if you are an athlete clothes that are more comfortable to move in will suit you better.
